Welcome to Spinlife New Zealand, here you can purchase spinning toys, and find spinning events happening in New Zealand.

Spinlife New Zealand promotes healthy lifestyle through the use of spinning props.
Spinning props regularly can improve reflex time, hand-eye coordination, muscle tone, improved multi-tasking ability, and it is well established that exercise releases seritonin so spinning really can lead to happiness.
